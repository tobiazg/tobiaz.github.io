About me

I like to say that I create visuals. Not only that it sounds better than saying that I am an artist, but it also sums up all the products I can provide for my clients. The range of these products is quite wide. As of 2024, I can provide:

Complex visual identities

Graphic design ranging from corporate logos to more punk and artistic posters.

Tattoos... Not only designs but I do the actual tattoos.

Photoshoots for brands or personal modeling portfolios.

Documenting (video/photo) music shows is one of those things that got me the most publicity out of all the things that I provide.

Video edits are mostly for brands or events that need online marketing.

Music videos are probably the most demanded "product" right now. As of 2024, I have done 5 so far.

Designing collections for fashion brands and overall making collaborations with brands that I personally enjoy might be my favorite form of "working".

I started out with art and creating all sorts of things when I was very young. Throughout the years I discovered many different platforms through which I could spill out all the ideas I had on my mind. Whether it was photography or graffiti in my teens I always had this urge to create something. It is very hard to describe on paper or just put it into words. Probably the best way to describe it is with a rule that I created five years ago when I was 16. "Every single day something new has to be created" There has not been a single day without obeying this one rule. Even when i was sick, in a hospital, abroad, sad, drunk... I made sure that every single day, I put out an idea that wasn't there before.
